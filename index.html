About KN Chemizol

Founded in 2008, KN Chemizol emerged from a vision to revolutionize the lubricant additives industry through innovation, quality, and customer-centric solutions. What began as a small research laboratory has evolved into a globally recognized leader in advanced additive formulation. Our journey has been marked by continuous investment in cutting-edge technology, strategic partnerships with industry leaders, and an unwavering commitment to exceeding customer expectations. Today, we serve over 500 lubricant manufacturers across six continents, providing them with the chemical solutions they need to compete in an increasingly demanding global market.

At the heart of KN Chemizol lies a mission to empower lubricant manufacturers with scientifically advanced, sustainable, and cost-effective additive solutions. We believe in fostering long-term partnerships built on trust, transparency, and mutual success. Our core values—Innovation, Excellence, Integrity, and Sustainability—guide every decision we make. We are dedicated to pushing the boundaries of what's possible in lubricant technology while maintaining the highest standards of environmental responsibility and regulatory compliance. Our team of world-class chemists, engineers, and technical specialists work tirelessly to ensure that every additive package we deliver represents the pinnacle of performance and reliability.

We offer a comprehensive portfolio of high-performance additives including anti-wear (AW) agents, anti-oxidants, detergents, dispersants, viscosity modifiers, pour point depressants, and foam inhibitors. Our product range covers automotive, industrial, marine, and specialty lubricant applications. Each additive is formulated to the highest purity standards and undergoes rigorous quality testing to ensure consistent performance and compatibility with various base oil systems.

Full development cycles including stability testing, performance validation, and regulatory compliance documentation usually take 8-12 weeks. Our experienced team works closely with you throughout the process to ensure rapid iteration and timely delivery. For expedited projects, we offer accelerated development programs with dedicated resources.

All our additives are formulated and tested to comply with major international standards and regulations including API, ASTM, ISO, NOACK, and environmental regulations such as REACH and RoHS.
